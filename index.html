Revenue Cycle Management

Comprehensive RCM services to optimize your healthcare revenue cycle from patient registration to payment collection.

1

Patient Pre-Authorization & Eligibility Verification

A strong revenue cycle starts before care is delivered. Our pre-authorization and eligibility verification services reduce denials at the root level.


We ensure that:

- Patient insurance coverage is verified in real-time through payer portals and clearinghouses
- Prior authorization requirements for procedures, diagnostics, and surgeries are proactively handled
- Accurate benefit coverage and co-pay details are communicated to the patient
- Front-office delays and claim rejections due to eligibility issues are minimized
2

Medical Coding (ICD-10, CPT, HCPCS)

Correct medical coding is the backbone of a healthy RCM. Our certified coders (CPC, CCS, CCS-P) ensure accurate and compliant coding based on documentation.


We provide



- Specialty-specific coding support (e.g., Internal Medicine, Radiology, Surgery, Dermatology)
- ICD-10 diagnosis coding, CPT procedure coding, and HCPCS coding for supplies/services
- Regular internal audits and cross-functional QA checks
- Up-to-date coding aligned with payer guidelines and NCCI edits
📊 Benefit: Reduced denials due to coding errors and maximized reimbursements.
3

Charge Entry & Claims Submission

We ensure timely and accurate charge capture and clean claim submission, reducing the revenue cycle time.


Services include:



- Double-verification of demographic and service data from EMRs/EHRs - CPT/HCPCS mapping with provider notes - Electronic submission via clearinghouses with rejection scrubbing - Paper claim submission where required (Medicaid, WC, etc.) 🚀 Benefit: Quick claim processing, reduced payer rejections, and shorter AR cycles.
4

Payment Posting (ERA & EOB)

We handle both automated (ERA) and manual (EOB) payment posting with precision.


Key tasks:



- Posting insurance payments, adjustments, co-pays, and deductibles accurately - Reconciling payments with bank deposits - Identifying underpayments, non-payments, and unprocessed claims - Flagging issues for follow-up and secondary billing 📈 Benefit: Real-time financial tracking, faster resolution of payment issues.
5

Accounts Receivable (AR) Follow-up

Outstanding claims are actively pursued by our AR experts through calls, portals, and email follow-ups.


AR Services include:



- Insurance AR aging reports (30/60/90/120+) - Aggressive follow-up on unresolved or delayed claims - Escalation of unresolved cases with payers - Patient balance collections with empathy and compliance 🔁 Benefit: Increased collections, minimized aging AR, improved cash flow.
6

Denial Management & Appeals

We specialize in identifying patterns behind denials and fixing them systematically.


What we offer:



- Categorization of denials (technical, clinical, authorization, etc.) - Development of appeal letters with medical necessity documentation - Continuous feedback loop to reduce recurring denials - Real-time denial dashboards for root cause analytics 🔍 Benefit: Recovery of lost revenue and denial prevention over time.
7

Provider Credentialing & Enrollment

We simplify the complex and time-consuming credentialing process to get providers paid faster.


Credentialing Services include:



- New provider enrollment with Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial payers - CAQH setup and maintenance - Re-credentialing and status tracking - Group practice enrollment, NPI registration, and EFT/ERA setups 🧾 Benefit: Quicker payer approvals, uninterrupted claims processing.
8

RCM Analytics & Business Intelligence Dashboards

We provide real-time performance tracking of your revenue cycle through interactive dashboards.


Insights provided:



- Collection rates, denial rates, and reimbursement timelines - Provider-wise performance reports - Payer-wise revenue mapping - Customized dashboards with visual KPIs 📉 Benefit: Data-driven decision-making and operational transparency.
9

HIPAA Compliance & Data Security

Data privacy and regulatory compliance are non-negotiable for us.


Our compliance measures include:



- HIPAA-trained staff and ongoing compliance education - Role-based access to protected health information (PHI) - Secure VPNs, encrypted file transfers, and monitored logins - Regular compliance audits and incident response protocols 🔐 Benefit: Risk mitigation, trust-building with patients and partners.
